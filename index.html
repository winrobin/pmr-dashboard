▶1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR？

PMR (Property Management Rights) is a uniquely Australian business model, most common in Queensland. You buy the right to manage a residential complex — not the physical property. PMR（物业管理权）是澳大利亚独特的商业模式，在昆州最常见。你购买的是管理权——不是房产本身。

  • Caretaking物业管理 — maintaining common areas (gardens, pools, BBQ)— 维护公共区域
  • Letting租赁管理 — managing rentals for unit owners— 为业主管理出租
💡 Income comes from BC salary (fixed) + letting commission (variable) 收入来自BC固定薪资 + lettings佣可变
