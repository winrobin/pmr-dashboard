▶1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR

PMR (Property Management Rights) is a uniquely Australian business model (Queensland most common). You buy the management agreement — NOT the property.

  • Caretaking — maintaining common areas (gardens, pools, BBQ)
  • Letting — managing rentals for unit owners
💡 Income = BC salary (fixed) + letting commission (variable)

Three Types

TypeWhat You BuyPrice Range
Business OnlyCaretaking + Letting agreement$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitPMR biz + manager apartment$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdPMR biz + own a unit$2M-$5M+

Permanent vs Holiday

  • Permanent — 6-12 month leases, stable income, lower returns
  • Holiday — Airbnb-style, higher returns, more work
  • Mixed — both types
▶2. Key Metrics2. 关键指标

① BC% — Most Important

BC% = BC Salary / Net Income × 100
BC%MeaningAction
50-70%Healthy✅ Good
70-80%Leaning caretaker⚠️ Caution
>80%Caretaker trap!❌ Avoid

② Multiplier (Price / Net Income)

Multiplier = Price / Net Profit
3-5xExcellent
5-6xFair
>6xOverpriced

③ $/wk Commission

$/wk = (Net Income – BC Salary) / Pool / 52
$60-85/wkNormal
$40-60/wkBelow avg
<$40/wkAbnormally low
▶3. Financial Analysis3. 财务分析

ROI

ROI = (Net Income / Price) × 100
15-25%Strong return
10-15%Average
<10%Weak
💡 BC salary only grows by CPI. Letting income grows with occupancy + rates + pool expansion!
