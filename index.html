Guide指南
▶1. What is PMR?1.什么是PMR?

PMR — uniquely Australian, QLD dominant. You buy the agreement — NOT the property.

  • Caretaking物业管理: maintaining common areas
  • Letting租赁管理: managing rentals for owners

Three Types三种类型

TypeWhatPrice
Business OnlyCaretaking + Letting$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitPMR biz + apartment$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdPMR biz + own unit$2M-$5M+
▶2. Key Metrics2.关键指标

BC% = BC Salary / Net Income × 100

50-70%Ideal — healthy balance
70-80%Caution — leaning caretaker
>80%🚨 Caretaker trap! AVOID

Multiplier = Price / Net Profit

3-5xExcellent — recoup in 3-5yr
5-6xFair market
>6xOverpriced

$/wk = (Net − BC) / Pool / 52

$60-85/wkNormal
$40-60/wkBelow avg
<$40/wk🚨 Too low!
▶3. Financial3.财务分析
Cash = Net − Mortgage

$800K, 70% LVR @ 6% = $33K/yr → $128K-$33K=$95K free cash

💡 BC grows by CPI only (~2-3%/yr). Letting GROWS with occupancy!
