1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR？

Property Management Rights (PMR) is a uniquely Australian business model, dominant in Queensland. You buy the right to manage a residential or holiday complex — you are buying the agreement, not the real estate.物业管理权（PMR）是澳大利亚独有的商业模式，在昆士兰州最为普遍。你购买的是管理住宅或度假综合体的权利——买的是协议，不是房产。

2. How the Business Works2. 商业运作方式

Your income comes from two streams: a Body Corporate (BC) salary for caretaking duties, and letting commissions from managing rentals on behalf of unit owners.收入来自两部分：物业委员会（BC）支付的物业管理薪资，以及代业主管理出租的租赁佣金。

Net Income = BC Salary + Letting Commissions − Expenses

Body Corporate SalaryBC薪资

  • Fixed annual salary paid by the BC for grounds, pool, common areasBC支付的固定年薪，用于花园、泳池、公共区域维护
  • Indexed annually — usually CPI or 3.5%, whichever is greater每年按CPI或3.5%（取较高者）调整
  • BC% = this salary as % of total net income. Ideal: 50–70%BC% = 该薪资占净收入百分比，理想：50–70%

Letting Income租赁收入

  • Earned by managing units in the "letting pool" — owners who authorise you通过管理"出租池"中的单元获得——即授权你出租的业主
  • Typical commission: 8–12% of rent collected典型佣金：收取租金的8–12%
  • Growth lever: recruit more units into your pool增长方式：争取更多单元加入出租池
💡 The BC salary is your floor — it pays your base even during slow periods. Letting income is where you grow.💡 BC薪资是收入底线，即使在淡季也能保障基本收入；租赁收入是你增长的来源。

5. Due Diligence Checklist5. 尽职调查清单

Before Making an Offer出价前

  • ☐ Request full information memorandum (IM) from broker☐ 向经纪人索取完整信息备忘录（IM）
  • ☐ Confirm Net Income is audited (not just stated)☐ 确认净收入已经过审计（非自报）
  • ☐ Get letting pool schedule — how many units, which owners☐ 获取出租池明细——单元数及业主名单
  • ☐ Check units managed by outside agents (leakage you can recover)☐ 检查由外部中介管理的单元（可争取回来的收入）
  • ☐ Confirm BC salary and annual CPI indexation clause☐ 确认BC薪资及年度CPI调整条款
  • ☐ Review BC meeting minutes (last 2 years) for disputes☐ 查阅过去2年BC会议记录，了解潜在纠纷
  • ☐ Confirm agreement term remaining and top-up history☐ 确认协议剩余年限及续约历史

After Signing (Due Diligence Period)签约后（尽职调查期）

  • ☐ Engage a PMR-specialist solicitor (not a general property lawyer)☐ 聘请PMR专业律师（非普通房产律师）
  • ☐ Engage a PMR accountant to verify all financials☐ 聘请PMR会计师核实财务
  • ☐ Request 2-year trust account bank statements☐ 索取信托账户2年银行对账单
  • ☐ Verify all letting agreements are current and signed☐ 核实所有出租协议均现行有效
  • ☐ Inspect condition of common areas — pool, gym, lifts, gardens☐ 检查公共区域状况——泳池、健身房、电梯、花园
  • ☐ Meet the BC committee members in person☐ 与BC委员会成员面谈
💡 Use a solicitor who specialises in MR — there are only ~10 firms in QLD who truly understand PMR contracts. Ask your broker for a referral.💡 务必使用MR专业律师——昆士兰只有约10家律所真正了解PMR合同。

7. Red Flags 🚨7. 风险警示 🚨

Skip immediately if any of these apply:出现以下任何一项立即跳过：
  • 🚨 BC% >85% — you are an employee, not a business owner你是雇员，不是生意老板
  • 🚨 Contract <10yr合同<10年 — no top-up in progress且无续约计划
  • 🚨 Mult >8x — no clear growth story且无明确增长逻辑
  • 🚨 Pool <5 units — too concentrated, high vacancy risk过于集中，空置风险极高
  • 🚨 100% BC salary100% BC薪资 — zero letting income = zero growth零租赁收入 = 零增长
  • 🚨 Projected income预测收入 — must be audited actuals only必须是经审计的实际数据
Investigate before proceeding:以下情况需仔细调查：
  • Price undisclosed (EOI/—) — ask why before requesting IM价格未披露（EOI/—）——索取IM前先了解原因
  • Large number of external-agent units — income leakage大量单元由外部中介管理——收入流失

9. Real Operator Experiences9. 从业者真实经验

These insights come from active PMR managers, brokers, and buyers across Queensland forums, industry groups, and published guides. They reflect real patterns — not hypotheticals.以下经验来自昆士兰州各论坛、行业群体及出版指南中的在职管理权经营者、经纪人和买家，反映的是真实规律，不是理论。

💡 Running the Business Day-to-Day日常经营

  • Phone never stops. Residents call at all hours — burst pipes at 11pm, locked out at 6am. New operators consistently underestimate the after-hours demand. If you value quiet evenings, budget for an after-hours call service (~$200–$400/mo).电话永远不会停。住户随时都会打来——晚上11点爆管、早上6点锁门。新手经营者往往低估了非工作时间的需求。如果你重视安静的夜晚，考虑安排接听服务（约$200–400/月）。
  • Your relationship with the BC committee is everything. A supportive committee makes life easy; a hostile one can make every year stressful. Visit the complex and meet committee members before you buy — not after.与BC委员会的关系至关重要。支持你的委员会让一切顺畅；对立的委员会会让每一年都很煎熬。在买之前去拜访综合体并与委员会成员见面——不要等到之后。
  • "The money is made in the letting, not the caretaking." Your BC salary is the floor; your real income growth comes from recruiting more units into your pool. Experienced managers actively court external-agent units by being responsive and competitive on fees."钱是在出租中赚到的，不是在物业管理中。"你的BC薪资是底线；真正的收入增长来自招募更多单元进入你的出租池。有经验的经营者会积极争取那些使用外部中介的单元。
  • Trust account discipline is non-negotiable. Every dollar of rent collected must flow through a separate trust account and be audited annually. Several managers have lost their licence over sloppy trust accounting — get proper software (PropertyMe, Console) from day one.信托账户管理不容妥协。收取的每一分租金都必须通过独立信托账户流转，并每年审计一次。一些经营者因信托账务混乱而吊销牌照——从第一天起就使用专业软件（PropertyMe、Console）。
  • Staff or no staff? Many smaller complexes (<50 units) are run owner-operator with no staff. Above ~80 units most managers bring in a part-time assistant. Factor this into your NI assessment — if the current operator has no staff but the complex clearly needs one, the stated NI is artificially high.要不要员工？很多小型综合体（<50单元）由业主兼职自营，没有员工。超过约80单元后，大多数经营者会聘用兼职助手。这需要纳入你的NI评估——如果当前经营者没有员工但综合体明显需要，则报告的NI是人为偏高的。

💰 On Pricing & Negotiation定价与议价

  • Vendors always lead with projected income. Always ask: "Is this audited actuals, or a projection?" — a vendor saying "it's on track to do $X" is not the same as two years of verified accounts. Walk away from projected-only listings.卖家总是拿预测收入说事。务必问清楚："这是经审计的实际收入，还是预测？"——卖家说"预计能做到$X"和两年经核实账目完全不是一回事。拒绝只有预测数据的物业。
  • Multiplier is negotiable — especially when metrics are mixed. If BC% is high (>75%) or contract is short (<15yr), you have genuine grounds to push the multiple down. Brokers price for the best case; you buy for the realistic case.倍数是可以议价的——尤其是当指标参差不齐时。如果BC%偏高（>75%）或合同偏短（<15年），你有实质理由压低倍数。经纪人按最好情况定价；你按现实情况购买。
  • Watch for "lifestyle premium" pricing. Coastal holiday complexes often carry a premium that pure numbers don't justify. It's fine if you want to live there — but don't confuse lifestyle with investment merit.警惕"生活方式溢价"定价。沿海度假综合体往往有纯数字无法支撑的溢价。如果你想住在那里，完全没问题——但别把生活方式和投资价值混为一谈。
  • Letting pool numbers can drift. A pool of 40 units sounds great — but if 8 have left in the past 12 months, the trajectory matters more than the snapshot. Ask for pool history, not just current count.出租池数量会变化。40个单元的出租池听起来不错——但如果过去12个月有8个离开了，趋势比快照更重要。要求查看出租池历史，而不仅仅是当前数量。

⚠️ Things That Caught People Off Guard让人措手不及的事

  • The BC can be your biggest risk. If you don't get along with the committee, they can make your life difficult: refuse top-ups, give you bad reviews to unit owners, or push to terminate for minor breaches. Culture fit matters as much as financials.BC可以是你最大的风险。如果你与委员会关系不好，他们可以让你日子难过：拒绝续约、向单元业主给你差评，或以小违规为由推动终止合同。文化契合和财务指标同样重要。
  • Capital works levies can destroy short-term cash flow. If the building has deferred maintenance (old lifts, roof issues, pool resurfacing), the BC can vote a special levy. You don't pay it — but owners do, and unhappy owners leave your letting pool.资本维修征费会破坏短期现金流。如果楼栋有积压维护（旧电梯、屋顶问题、泳池翻新），BC可以投票征收特别税。你不用交——但业主要交，不满意的业主会退出你的出租池。
  • Vacancy is contagious. One empty unit doesn't hurt much; five empty units in a holiday complex during the off-season can make your commissions drop 30–40% in a quarter. Holiday PMR needs a cash buffer of at least 3 months of living expenses.空置具有连锁效应。一个空置单元影响不大；度假综合体淡季五个空置单元可能让你的佣金一个季度下降30–40%。度假PMR至少需要3个月生活费的现金储备。
  • "I wish I'd met the outgoing manager earlier." Sitting down with the seller for a full handover (reservation system, maintenance contacts, quirky residents, BC dynamics) is invaluable — negotiate for a 1–2 week handover period in your contract."我希望能早点与前任经营者见面。"与卖家坐下来进行完整的交接（预订系统、维护联系人、特殊住户情况、BC动态）非常宝贵——在合同中争取1–2周的交接期。
💡 Biggest consensus across operators: Spend twice as long on the BC relationship and financials as you think you need to. The technical side of running a complex is learnable in weeks; undoing a bad deal or a hostile BC takes years.💡 从业者最大的共识：在BC关系和财务核查上花两倍于你认为需要的时间。运营综合体的技术层面几周内就能学会；但要解开一笔糟糕的交易或扭转敌对的BC关系，可能需要好几年。

10. Licences, Certificates & Documents10. 牌照、证书与所需材料

  • ☐ Last 2–3 years of audited profit & loss statements☐ 过去2–3年经审计的损益表
  • ☐ Last 2 years of trust account bank statements☐ 过去2年信托账户银行对账单
  • ☐ Current letting pool schedule (list of all managed units + owner names)☐ 当前出租池明细（所有管理单元及业主名单）
  • ☐ Copies of all current letting agreements (signed)☐ 所有当前出租协议的副本（已签署）
  • ☐ Management agreement (caretaking agreement with BC)☐ 管理协议（与BC的物业管理协议）
  • ☐ Letting agreement (the agreement governing your letting rights)☐ 出租协议（管理你出租权利的协议）
  • ☐ Confirmation of remaining contract term☐ 合同剩余年限确认书
  • ☐ Last 2 years of BC meeting minutes and financials☐ 过去2年BC会议记录及财务报告
  • ☐ Schedule of BC levies (admin + sinking fund)☐ BC征费明细（行政费+维修基金）
  • ☐ List of approved contractors / maintenance suppliers☐ 已批准承包商/维护供应商名单

From the Body Corporate来自物业委员会（BC）

  • ☐ Consent to assignment of management rights (BC resolution)☐ 管理权转让同意书（BC决议）
  • ☐ BC certificate confirming no outstanding breaches☐ 确认无未解决违规事项的BC证书
  • ☐ Sinking fund forecast report☐ 维修基金预测报告
  • ☐ Building & contents insurance certificates☐ 楼栋及财产保险证书

From the Office of Fair Trading来自公平交易办公室（OFT）

  • ☐ Approved RLA Licence (your name, attached to this complex)☐ 已批准的RLA牌照（你的名字，附属于该综合体）
  • ☐ If applicable: company trust account setup documentation☐ 如适用：公司信托账户设置文件

Finance Documents (if borrowing)融资文件（如需贷款）

  • ☐ Formal loan approval letter from lender☐ 贷款机构出具的正式贷款批准函
  • ☐ Business valuation report (lender will require this for the MR business component)☐ 业务估值报告（贷款机构对MR业务部分的要求）
  • ☐ If buying MR+Unit: separate residential loan approval for unit☐ 如购买MR+公寓：公寓的独立住宅贷款批准
💡 Timeline tip: Allow 90–120 days from signing to settlement for PMR. The BC consent vote, OFT licence processing, and finance approval all take time and cannot happen in parallel. Work backward from your target settlement date to ensure nothing holds you up.💡 时间线建议：PMR从签约到交割预留90–120天。BC同意投票、OFT牌照审批和融资批准都需要时间，且不能并行处理。从你的目标交割日期倒推，确保没有任何环节拖慢进度。
