Guide
▶What is PMR?什么是PMR?

Property Management Rights (PMR) — uniquely Australian. Buy the agreement, NOT the property.

  • Caretaking: Maintain common areas (gardens, pool, BBQ, cleaning)
  • Letting租赁管理: Manage rentals for unit owners, earn commission

Three Types

TypeWhatPrice
Business OnlyCaretaking + Letting only$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitPMR + manager apartment$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdPMR + own the unit$2M-$5M+
▶How to Buy购买流程

Phase 1: Prep (Wks 1-4)

  1. Set budget (30-40% deposit needed)
  2. Choose type (holiday vs permanent)
  3. Build team (broker, financier, accountant, lawyer)
  4. Get pre-approved

Phase 2: Search (Wks 5-12)

  1. Broker shows listings matching criteria
  2. Focus on: BC% 60-70%, mult 3-5x, contract 10yr+
  3. Inspect complex, manager unit, common areas
  4. Submit offer

Phase 3: Due Diligence

  • Accountant: Verify all income figures independently
  • Lawyer: Review care-taking agreement, letting agreement, BC by-laws, past minutes
  • Physical: Pest/building, pool condition, maintenance backlog
  • Letting Pool: Count units, check stability, growth potential
  • BC Relationship: Meet committee, review minutes for disputes

    • Phase 4: Approval & Settle (Wks 12-20)

    1. Final finance approval
    2. Body Corporate approval
    3. Get RLA licence (during settlement)
    4. Settlement - own the business!
▶Checklist尽职调查清单

Financial

  • Verify net income independently
  • Check: BC salary, letting commissions, pool fees, cleaning
  • 3+ years financial statements
  • Trust account records
  • Occupancy rates (perm 95%+, holiday 60-70%)

Legal

  • Care-taking agreement (duties, pay, term, renewal)
  • Letting agreement (commission, terms)
  • BC by-laws + 2 years minutes
  • Outstanding disputes or legal claims

Physical

  • Building/pest inspection on unit
  • Pool, gym, BBQ, common areas
  • Maintenance backlog? Major upcoming expenditure?

Letting Pool

  • Count current pool units
  • Perm vs holiday? Growth potential?
  • Risk of losing units? Outside agents?
▶Pitfalls常见陷阱
Inadequate DD - #1 reason PMR purchases fail. Never rely on seller's figures. Pay $3-5K for accountant verification.
Poor BC relationship - BC makes your life good or bad. Review minutes carefully for disputes.
Outdated agreements - Ensure duties and pay clearly defined. Can BC add work without more pay?
Losing pool units - Owners can leave pool, live in unit, or use outside agents. Check stability.
Underestimate workload - Perm: ~15-20 hrs/wk. Holiday: ~25-40 hrs/wk. Be realistic.
Wrong advisors - Use only PMR-specialised accountant and lawyer. Generalists miss critical issues.
▶Tips心得

Rules:

  • 30/40 Rule: 30-40% deposit for most loans
  • Multiplier: 3-5x good, >6x overpriced
  • BC%: 50-70% ideal, >80% caretaker trap
  • Caretaking pay: Must cover your time + profit margin

Questions for Seller:

  • Current BC relationship?
  • How many units in pool? How long have they been there?
  • Any considering leaving pool?
  • Major upcoming maintenance/costs?
  • Hours per week workload?
  • Any disputes with owners?

Best resources: AccomProperties.com.au, ResortBrokers.com.au, TheOnsiteManager.com.au

Pro Tip: Talk to CURRENT or FORMER PMR owners. They give real picture, not broker's polished version.
