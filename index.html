Guide指南
▶1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR?

PMR (Property Management Rights) — uniquely Australian, QLD dominant. You buy the agreement — NOT the property.

  • Caretaking物业管理: maintaining common areas维护公共区域
  • Letting租赁管理: managing rentals for owners为业主管理出租

Three Types三种类型

Type类型What内容Price价格
Business Only纯商业Caretaking + Letting管理+租赁$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitMR+公寓PMR biz + apartmentPMR + 公寓$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdMR+产权PMR + own unitPMR + 拥有unit$2M-$5M+
▶2. Key Metrics2. 关键指标

BC%

BC% = BC Salary / Net Income × 100
BC%
50-70%Healthy balance理想
70-80%Leaning caretaker注意
>80%🚨 Caretaker trap!纯caretaker陷阱

Multiplier

Mult = Price / Net Profit
3-5xExcellent极佳
5-6xFair合理
>6xOverpriced过高

$/wk per unit

$/wk = (Net − BC Salary) / Pool / 52
$60-85Normal
$40-60Below avg
<$40Too low!
▶3. Financial Analysis3. 财务分析

ROI

ROI = (Net / Price) × 100
15-25%Strong
10-15%Average
<10%Weak

Leveraged Return杠杆回报

Cash = Net − Mortgage
$128K − $33K = $95K
💡 BC salary grows by CPI only (~2-3%/yr). Letting income GROWS with occupancy, rates, and pool expansion!💡 BC薪资只随CPI增长（~2-3%/年）。Letting收入随入住率和扩展增长！
