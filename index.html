▶1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR?

PMR (Property Management Rights) is a uniquely Australian business model — most common in Queensland. You buy the agreement (the right to manage) — NOT the property.

  • Caretaking物业管理: maintaining common areas维护公共区域
  • Letting租赁管理: managing rentals for owners为业主管理出租
💡 You buy the business agreement, not the physical property.你购买的是经营权协议，不是房产本身。

Three Types三种类型

Type类型What You Buy购买内容Price价格
Business Only纯商业Caretaking + Letting管理+租赁$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitMR+公寓PMR biz + apartmentPMR业务 + 公寓$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdMR+产权PMR biz + own a unitPMR业务 + 拥有unit$2M-$5M+

Permanent vs Holiday长租 vs 短租

  • Permanent长租: 6-12 month leases, stable, lower returns6-12个月，收入稳定
  • Holiday短租: Airbnb-style, higher returns, more workAirbnb式，回报高，工作多
  • Mixed混合: both types两者结合
▶2. Key Metrics2. 关键指标

① BC% — Most Important① BC% — 最重要的指标

BC% = BC Salary / Net Income × 100
BC%Meaning含义Action行动
50-70%Healthy理想✅ Good
70-80%leaning caretaker注意⚠️ Caution
>80%🚨 Caretaker trap!纯caretaker陷阱❌ Avoid!
🚨 BC >80% means no real letting business — you can't grow income, only get fixed salary.BC >80%意味着没有真正的letting业务。收入只有固定薪资——无法增长。

② Multiplier② 倍数

Multiplier = Price / Net Profit
3-5xExcellent极佳
5-6xFair合理
>6xOverpriced过高

③ $/wk per unit③ 每单元周佣金

$/wk = (Net Income – BC Salary) / Pool / 52
$60-85Normal正常
$40-60Below avg偏低
<$40🚨 Abnormally low!极低！
▶3. Financial Analysis3. 财务分析

ROI投资回报率

ROI = (Net Income / Price) × 100
15-25%Strong强
10-15%Average平均
<10%Weak弱

Leveraged Return杠杆回报

Cash Flow = Net Income − Annual Mortgage

Example: $800K, 70% LVR @ 6% = $33K/yr mortgage示例：$800K，70%LVR@6%=$33K/年

$128K − $33K = $95K free cash flow净现金流
💡 BC salary grows by CPI only (~2-3%/yr). Letting income GROWS with occupancy, rates, and pool expansion!BC薪资只随CPI增长（~2-3%/年）。Letting收入随入住率和扩展增长！
