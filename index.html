Guide: Buying PMR
▶What is PMR?什么是PMR？

Property Management Rights (PMR) is a uniquely Australian business model — most common in Queensland. You buy the agreement to manage a strata complex, NOT the building itself.物业管理权(PMR)是澳大利亚独特的商业模式，最常见于昆州。你购买管理协议，而不是建筑本身。

Caretaking: Maintain common areas — gardens, pools, BBQ, cleaning物业管理: 维护公共区域 — 花园、泳池、烧烤区、清洁

Letting: Manage rentals for unit owners — earn commission on each rental租赁管理: 为业主管理出租 — 从每次租赁中赚取佣金

Three Types三种类型

Type类型What内容Price价格
Business Only纯商业Caretaking + Letting only仅管理+租赁$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitMR+公寓PMR business + manager apartmentPMR业务+经理公寓$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdMR+产权PMR + own the propertyPMR+拥有产权$2M-$5M+
▶How to Buy (Step-by-Step)购买流程

Phase 1: Preparation (Weeks 1-4)阶段1：准备（第1-4周）

1. Research — understand what PMR entails, define budget, choose holiday vs permanent1. 调研 — 了解PMR内容、确定预算、选择短租或长租

2. Build your team — specialist broker, PMR financier, PMR accountant, PMR lawyer2. 组建团队 — 专业经纪人、PMR贷款专家、PMR会计师、PMR律师

3. Get pre-approved for finance (30-40% deposit typically required)3. 贷款预批准（通常需要30-40%首付）

Phase 2: Search & Evaluate (Weeks 5-12)阶段2：搜索与评估（第5-12周）

4. Your broker presents matching listings. Focus on BC% 60-70%, multiplier 3-5x, contract 10yr+4. 经纪人推荐匹配的挂牌。关注BC% 60-70%、倍数3-5x、合同10年以上

5. Submit offer once you find a match. Contract includes conditions for inspections, due diligence, finance, BC approval5. 找到合适的提交报价。合同包含检查、尽调、贷款、BC批准条件

Phase 3: Due Diligence (CRITICAL!)阶段3：尽职调查（关键！）

  • Accountant: Independently verify ALL income — BC salary, letting commissions, pool fees, cleaning. Check 3+ years financial statements, trust account records, occupancy rates会计师: 独立验证所有收入 — BC薪资、租赁佣金、泳池费用、清洁。检查3年以上财务报表、信托账户记录、入住率
  • Lawyer: Review care-taking agreement (duties, pay, term, renewal), letting agreement, BC by-laws, past minutes, disputes律师: 审查管理协议（职责、薪酬、期限、续约）、租赁协议、BC规约、过往会议记录、纠纷
  • Physical inspections: Building/pest inspection on manager unit, check pool/gym/BBQ condition, check maintenance backlog物理检查: 经理单元的建筑/白蚁检查、检查泳池/健身房/BBQ状态、检查维护积压
  • Letting Pool: Count pool units, check stability, check for units likely to leave租赁池: 统计池中单元、检查稳定性、检查可能离开的单元
  • BC Relationship: Meet the Body Corporate committee. Good relationship is essential for long-term successBC关系: 与业主委员会见面。良好的关系对长期成功至关重要

Phase 4: Approval & Settlement (Weeks 12-20)阶段4：批准与交割（第12-20周）

6. Final finance approval6. 最终贷款批准

7. Body Corporate approval (BC must approve you as manager)7. 业主批准（BC必须批准你为经理）

8. Get RLA licence (during settlement period)8. 获取RLA执照（在交割期间）

9. Settlement — you now own the business!9. 交割 — 你现在拥有这个业务！

▶Due Diligence Checklist尽职调查清单

Financial财务

  • Verify net income independently独立验证净收入
  • Check ALL income sources: BC salary, commissions, pool fees, cleaning检查所有收入来源：BC薪资、佣金、泳池费用、清洁
  • 3+ years financial statements3年以上财务报表
  • Trust account records信托账户记录
  • Occupancy rates (perm 95%+, holiday 60-70% avg)入住率（长租95%+，短租平均60-70%）

Legal法律

  • Care-taking agreement: duties, responsibilities, remuneration, term, renewal options, CPI increases管理协议：职责、责任、报酬、期限、续约选项、CPI增长
  • Letting agreement: commission rates, terms租赁协议：佣金率、条款
  • BC by-laws + 2 years of meeting minutes (look for disputes)BC规约+2年会议记录（查找纠纷）
  • Any outstanding disputes or legal claims任何未决纠纷或法律索赔

Letting Pool租赁池

  • Count current pool units统计当前 pool unit 数量
  • Composition: permanent vs holiday?组成：长租vs短租？
  • Growth potential? Risk of losing units?增长潜力？失去单元的风险？
▶Common Pitfalls (READ CAREFULLY)常见陷阱（仔细阅读）
Inadequate Due Diligence尽职调查不足
#1 reason PMR purchases fail. Never rely on seller's figures. Pay $3-5K for specialist PMR accountant verification.PMR购买失败的主要原因。永远不要依赖卖家的数据。花$3-5K请专业PMR会计师验证。
Poor BC RelationshipBC关系差
The BC committee can make your life beautiful or terrible. Review minutes carefully for disputes, personality clashes, unresolved issues.BC委员会可以让你生活美好或糟糕。仔细检查会议记录中的纠纷、人格冲突、未决问题。
Ambiguous or Outdated Agreements模糊或过时的协议
Poorly written care-taking agreements can mean more work for the same pay. Can BC increase workload without more pay?写得不好的管理协议可能意味着同样的工资更多的工作。BC能在不加薪的情况下增加工作量吗？
Loss of Letting Pool Units失去租赁池单元
Units can leave if owners decide to live in the unit, use outside agents, or leave them empty.如果业主决定自住、使用外部代理或空置，单元会离开。
Underestimate Workload低估工作量
Permanent: ~15-20 hrs/week. Holiday: ~25-40 hrs/week. Be realistic about daily responsibilities.长租：~每周15-20小时。短租：~每周25-40小时。对日常职责要现实。
Wrong Advisors选错顾问
Using a regular accountant or lawyer who hasn't done PMR deals is a major risk. They WILL miss critical issues.使用没有做过PMR交易的普通会计师或律师是重大风险。他们会遗漏关键问题。
▶Key Rules & Tips关键规则与技巧

30/40 Rule: 30-40% deposit required for most PMR loans30/40规则: 大多数PMR贷款需要30-40%首付

Multiplier Rule: 3-5x = excellent value, 5-6x = fair market, >6x = overpriced倍数规则: 3-5x = 极佳价值，5-6x = 市场公平，>6x = 定价过高

BC Ratio Rule: 50-70% = healthy, 70-80% = caution, >80% = caretaker trap (AVOID!)BC比率规则: 50-70% = 健康，70-80% = 注意，>80% = caretaker陷阱（避免！）

Pro Tip: Talk to CURRENT or FORMER PMR owners. They give you the real picture — not the broker's polished version.专家提示: 与现任或前任PMR业主交谈。他们会给你真实的画面 — 而不是经纪人的美化版本。
