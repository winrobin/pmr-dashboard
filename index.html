▶1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR?

PMR (Property Management Rights) — uniquely Australian, most common in QLD. You buy the agreement — NOT the property.

  • Caretaking物业管理: maintaining common areas维护公共区域
  • Letting租赁管理: managing rentals for owners为业主管理出租

Three Types三种类型

Type类型What内容Price价格
Business Only纯商业Caretaking + Letting管理+租赁$500K-$1.5M
MR + UnitMR+公寓PMR + apartmentPMR + 公寓$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdMR+产权PMR + own unitPMR + 拥有unit$2M-$5M+

BC%

BC% = BC Salary / Net Income × 100

Mult = Price / Net Profit

>6xOverpriced过高

$/wk = (Net − BC Salary) / Pool / 52
