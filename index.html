▶ 1. What is PMR?1. 什么是PMR？

PMR (Property Management Rights) is a uniquely Australian business model, most common in Queensland. It combines: PMR（物业管理权）是澳大利亚独特的商业模式，在昆州最常见。它结合：

  • Caretaking物业管理员 — maintaining common areas (gardens, pools, BBQ, laundry)— 维护公共区域
  • Letting租赁管理 — managing short-term or long-term rentals for unit owners— 为业主管理短租或长租
💡 You buy the agreement (the right to manage), NOT the physical property.你购买的是经营权协议，不是房产本身。

Three Types三种类型

Type类型What You Buy购买内容Price Range价格范围
Business Only纯商业Caretaking + Letting only管理+租赁协议$500K-$1.5M
MR + Unit (MLU)MR+公寓PMR business + manager apartmentPMR业务+经理公寓$1M-$3M+
MR + FreeholdMR+产权PMR business + own a unitPMR业务+拥有unit$2M-$5M+

Permanent vs Holiday长租 vs 短租

  • Permanent长租 — 6-12 month leases, stable income, lower returns— 6-12个月租约，收入稳定，回报较低
  • Holiday短租 — Airbnb-style, higher returns, more work— Airbnb式，回报更高，工作更多
  • Mixed混合 — both types (most versatile, most complex)— 两者结合（功能多但复杂）
